Rafael Araneda - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-08

TV show host who is best know for his work on Chilevisión. He won the Bravo Award for Best Television Driver through the National Association of Actors in 2010. He has 850,000 followers on his rafaaraneda Instagram account. 

Before Fame

He worked for TOP 30 on La Red from 1994 to 1996. 

Trivia

He became lead of International Song Festival of Viña del Mar in 2011.

Family Life

He married Marcela Vacarezza in 2000. Their children are Vicente, Martina, and Florencia. 

Associated With

He and Tess Daly are TV hosts both born in 1969.
