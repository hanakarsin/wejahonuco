Three people shot dead and two others sustain gunshot wounds after NASA protesters stormed a police

Publish date: 2024-03-06

- Three people have lost their lives after being shot by police during a violent anti-IEBC demo in Bondo

- Two others have been left with serious gun shot wounds

- Hell broke loose after a group of protesters stormed a police station armed with crude weapons

Three people believed to be ardent NASA supporters have been shot dead during an anti-IEBC demo in Bondo,Siaya county.

TUKO.co.ke has learnt that the deceased were shot on Friday,October 13 ,after an anti-IEBC demo turned chaotic.

The three are said to have met their deaths after police officers opened fire to a group of protesters who had stormed a police station armed with crude weapons.

Two other people,a 70-year-old woman and a boda boda rider suffered serious bullet wounds after they were shot and injured during the same incident.The two were rushed to the Bondo sub-county hospital where they were admitted for treatment.

Confirming the incident,Area police boss Paul Kiarie said a crowd of irate protesters stormed the station armed with crude weapons during anti-IEBC protests,forcing officers to open fire.The shooting comes just a day after Interior CS Fred Matiang’i banned all anti-IEBC demos in Nairobi, Mombasa and Kisumu CBDs.

As reported by TUKO.co.ke earlier, Amnesty International, a human rights organization had warned NASA protesters of violent police crackdown following Matiang’i’s ban.
