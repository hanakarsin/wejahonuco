Young Dolph - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Rapper known for singles such as "Major" and "RNB." His seventh album, Rich Slave, was his most successful album, debuting at #4 on the Billboard 200 while peaking at #3 on the US Rap chart. 

Before Fame

He released his debut mixtape, Paper Route Campaign, in 2009. His first studio album King of Memphis dropped in 2016. 

Trivia

He founded the record label Paper Route Empire.

Family Life

He was born Adolph Thornton Jr. in Chicago, Illinois but raised in Memphis, Tennessee by his grandmother. He had four siblings, two sisters and two brothers. He and Juice WRLD were cousins. He was married to Mia Jaye and they had two children together, including Tre Tre Thornton.

Associated With

He worked exclusively with Gucci Mane on several projects.
