2021 Police Recruitment Portal Opens For Fresh Application

Publish date: 2024-04-13

As earlier announced in a statement signed by the Police Headquarters Public Relations Officer, Frank Mba, recruitment into the Nigerian Police Force starts today, November 29.

The force had noted that candidates would start receiving invitations for screening starting from 10th January 2022 upon successful upload of all the required information on the registration portal.

Naija News understands that the registration portal is only open for candidates interested in the Police Constables (recruits) cadre.

The portal would be open only for a period of six weeks starting from Monday, 29th November 2021 to Monday, 10th January 2021.

How to Apply: Interested persons are expected to fill the application only on the police recruitment portal – www.policerecruitment.gov.ng.

NOTE: Registration is FREE.

See below requirements and guidelines for possible enlistment.

– Minimum age required is – 17
– Maximum age required is 25
– Height should not be more than 1.67 while the minimum height required is 1.64
– Applicants must at least 5 credits in WASSCE or NECO including English and Mathematics.
– Applicants must be physically and mentally fit while female applicants must not be pregnant at the time of applying.
– Candidates are expected to print, fill their guarantor forms downloaded from the portal upon completion of their registration.

– All forms upon completion must be printed for proof during screening.
