Cuban Doll - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-11

Social media star turned rapper who is known for her Cubanndasavage Instagram account. She released the song "Rat B*tch" in July 2017, which accumulated over 30,000 views on YouTube within the first few days of its release.

Before Fame

She first joined Twitter in March of 2012. Upon signing up for Instagram, she created her account with the username Cubanndoll.

Trivia

She has amassed over 2 million followers on Instagram by sharing her professional modeling photos as well as daily lifestyle. 

Family Life

Her real name is Aaliyah Keef. She was born in Dallas, Texas, and has also lived in Atlanta, Georgia. 

Associated With

Her music video for "Rat B*tch" was shot by YouTuber Jmoney1041.
