Jack Scalfani - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-25

Youtube star known for posting food content. His popular videos include demos and tips like how to cut an onion without crying and the easiest way to peel an egg. He also includes product reviews for knives and other tools. He has amassed more than 500,000 subscribers on his Cooking With Jack Show channel.

Before Fame

He attended Cypress College and Fullerton College in California. He worked as a DJ at Country KIK FM and KEWB FM. He launched his YouTube channel in 2006.

Trivia

One of his most viewed videos demonstrates how to prepare "poor man's" filet mignon. He claims his technique makes a cheap steak taste delicious. He launched GiveMe Foods, a company that manufacturers and distributes sauces and gourmet seasoning.

Family Life

He has a son named Jack Jr. In March 2010, the pair joined forces to bake a big cupcake.

Associated With

He and Todd's Kitchen are both popular YouTubers who post food content.
