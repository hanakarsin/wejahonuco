Political parties registrar Anne Nderitu declines registering Tanga Tanga Movement

Publish date: 2024-03-02

Attempt by a group of politicians to register new political party dubbed Tanga Tanga Movement hit a snag on Tuesday, September 17, after they were turned them away.

The politicians had written to registrar of political parties Anne Nderitu on the matter and requested to have names The Tangatanga Movement and Tanga Tanga Movement reserved for them.

However in a terse response, Nderitu noted the names suggested did not comply to the Constitution particularly Political Parties Act 2011.

"Further to this and without prejudice upon registration of the party, the name shall not be in line with the Article 91 of the Constitution of Kenya. Kindly suggest other names for verification," she said.

Though name Tanga Tanga is associated with Deputy President William Ruto, it was not immediately established to whom the group owe allegiance.

Tanga Tanga is made up of renegade Jubilee politicians in support of Ruto's presidential bid.
