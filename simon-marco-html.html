Simon Marco - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Reality star and entrepreneur best recognized for making an appearance on season 1 of Summerhouse Martha's Vineyard. His Instagram account simonmarco_official has gained over 90,000 followers. 

Before Fame

He began posting content on Instagram in June 2014. He began his first online business at the age of 16 and in just two years made over $1 million.

Trivia

He is the brand manager at watchroom24. His brand sells luxury timepieces and jewelry across the globe. As a businessman and entrepreneur, he specializes in high fashion. 

Family Life

He is in a relationship with Bria Raven Fleming. They have a dog named Milo Fleming. He and Bria met at Cannes Film Festival. 

Associated With

In October 2019, he posted a photo alongside model Papis Loveday.
