Jonathan Le - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Instagram personality and social media influencer who rose to fame under the nickname Mini Mike. He shares lifestyle and family photos for his more than 460,000 Instagram followers.  He has a Youtube channel titled Jonathan's World. He has also done multiple dance videos alongside well known people like Jason Derulo. 

Before Fame

He began his social media career on TikTok but his account was banned/hacked in 2020.

Trivia

He has appeared in content alongside members of the Shluv House collective, and is also a member of The Shluv Family. In May of 2021 he posted a photo with Terry Crews on his Instagram and the post has been liked over 68 thousand times. 

Family Life

He is the younger brother of fellow influencer Michael Le, and also has another brother named Daniel and a sister named Tiffany. His mother's name is Tina Le. 

Associated With

He was photographed with Ondreaz Lopez at a pool party in 2020.
