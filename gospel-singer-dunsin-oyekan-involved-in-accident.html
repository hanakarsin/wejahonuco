Gospel Singer, Dunsin Oyekan, Involved In Accident

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Popular gospel singer, Dunsin Oyekan on Saturday survived a motor accident along the Lagos-Ibadan expressway.

Naija News learnt that Dunsin was involved in the accident while travelling from Lagos to Ibadan, the Oyo State capital.

According to reports, one of the singer’s crew members sustained an injury in the accident.

Dunsin, however, escaped unharmed in the life-threatening accident.

A further report had it that the gospel singer despite the accident still went ahead to minister at the program he was going to before the accident.

In other news, two operatives of the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission, (EFCC) reportedly assaulted a Nollywood actress, Helena Duru in her residence in Asaba.

Naija News reports that the president of the Actors Guild of Nigeria, Emeka Rollas, in a post on Instagram on Saturday condemned the actions of the operatives of the anti-graft agency on its member, Helena whilst demanding compensation.

The president had alleged that the operatives, some weeks ago, had pounced on the actress for refusing to open the corridor door for them to gain access into her compound.

He revealed the identities of two of the operatives who carried out the dastardly act on the actress as ADE and ABDULLAHI from the Enugu Zonal office.
