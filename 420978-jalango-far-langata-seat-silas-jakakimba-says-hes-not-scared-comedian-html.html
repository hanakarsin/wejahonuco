Jalang'o is Far from the Lang'ata MP Seat, Silas Jakakimba Says He's Not Scared of Comedian

Publish date: 2024-05-23
  • Silas Jakakimba said comedian Jalang'o did not have the resources to scare anyone in the Lang'ata parliamentary race
  • Both Jalang'o and Jakakimba are in the race seeking to unseat first-term Jubilee MP Nixon Korir
  • Recently, Jalang'o joined the Raila-led ODM party in readiness for the Lang'ata seat tussle

City Lawyer Silas Jakakimba has watered down comedian Felix Odiwuor alias Jalango's prospects of clinching the Lang'ata parliamentary seat.

Both Jalang'o and Jakakimba are in the race to unseat first-term Jubilee MP Nixon Korir in the forthcoming 2022 General Election.

Taking to Twitter on Monday, August 2, the lawyer, who doubles up as ODM leader Raila Odinga's aide insisted that the radio presenter was far away from clinching the seat.

While replying to lawyer Ahmednassir Abdullahi's question if he was scared of Jalang'o in the race, Jakakimba said the comedian did not have the wherewithal to scare anyone.

"The fisherfolk could do anything including going back to basics to fish on the promised but, my very close friend Jalang’o is far from The Lang’ata parliamentary seat. Separately, he would attest to you he does not have the wherewithal to scare anyone," said Jakakimba.

Jalang'o joins ODM

As previously reported, Jalang'o officially joined the Raila led Orange party in readiness for the Lang'ata seat tussle.

To welcome Jalang'o was ODM's secretary-general Edwin Sifuna who witnessed the comedian being handed the party's life membership certificate.

Jalang'o announced his bid in 2018 when he engaged his fans in a question and answer session on Instagram.

A fan had asked him what his dream was, and without hesitating, he said he was dreaming of becoming the Lang'ata MP in 2022.
