AG McDaniel - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-15

TikTok star who is known for sharing skit, dancing, and LGBTQ related videos and content for her over 1.9 million fans. She is also known for sharing unique edits and photos through her Instagram as well. She posted a TikTok to the Sam Smith song, "Like I Can". The video has been viewed over 1.3 million times. 

Before Fame

She first started sharing modeling based photos through her Instagram in January of 2018. Then in June of 2019, she started sharing skits and music driven videos through her TikTok account which quickly garnered attention. 

Trivia

She is also known for her passion in basketball and shared several photos of her playing on her high school team through her Instagram. She has a tattoo of a snake on her arm. 

Family Life

She was born in Albuquerque, New Mexico but has lived in Louisville, Kentucky. The AG in her name stands for Annagrace.

Associated With

She collaborated with fellow TikTok star Kailey Amora on a video which she shared in August of 2019 through the app.
