Kid X - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-17

South African Music Award-winning rapper who had three consecutive number ones on YFM’s Hot 9 Hip-Hop chart. He was also joined by AB Crazy and Smashis in the group Cashtime Fam before they disbanded in 2014.

Before Fame

His unorthodox rise came after having been recommended to Teargas’ K.O. by Smashis during the formation of Cashtime Fam.

Trivia

He was featured on the 2012 hit single "BoomShakaLaka" with Kwesta.

Family Life

He was born Bonginkosi Mahlangu to parents Florence Baphelile and Sam Mahlangu in Middleburg, South Africa.

Associated With

He and Cassper Nyovest are both award-winning South African rappers.
