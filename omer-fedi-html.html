Omer Fedi - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Guitarist, producer and songwriter who has worked with Lil Nas X, Yungblud and Machine Gun Kelly. He co-wrote 24KGoldn's 2020 track "Mood," which gained over 190 million views on YouTube.

Before Fame

He moved to Los Angeles with no connections in the music industry.

Trivia

He started playing the guitar at the age of ten.

Family Life

His father is Israeli drummer Asher Fedi. In 2021, he began dating TikTok star and actress Addison Rae. 

Associated With

He embraced jazz from listening to Walter Becker and Donald Fagen's band Steely Dan.
