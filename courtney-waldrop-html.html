Courtney Waldrop - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Matriarch of the Waldrop family, who are the stars of the TLC reality show Sweet Home Sextuplets. After struggling from multiple miscarriages, she tried fertility medication to assist further pregnancies. Although she was given a low dose, she was soon pregnant with sextuplets, doubling the size of her family. She is also very popular on Instagram, gaining 550,000 followers.

Before Fame

She was a first grade teacher at Albertville City Schools. 

Trivia

In August 2017, her local church held a gender reveal 5k marathon to help raise money for the family. She sells merchandise under the company Gods Divine Nine.

Family Life

She and her husband Eric have been together since the eighth grade, and were married in 2004. They have nine children - Saylor (2010), twins Wales and Bridge (2013), and the sextuplets, Rayne, Rawlings, Rivers, Tag, Layke, and Blu, born ten weeks prematurely in December 2017.

Associated With

Barb Putman and Michelle Duggar were also the matriarchs of reality shows about large families.
