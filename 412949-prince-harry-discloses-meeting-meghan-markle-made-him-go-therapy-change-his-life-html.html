Prince Harry Discloses Meeting Meghan Markle Made Him Go for Therapy, Change His Life

Publish date: 2024-05-30

- Prince Harry and Meghan Markle met in 2016 and wedded in May 2018

- While premiering on his mental health series he is producing with Oprah Winfrey, the royalty said meeting his wife changed his life

- According to him, he had to find ways of dealing with his trauma so as not to affect his relationship

The Duke of Sussex Prince Harry has opened up to media mogul Oprah Winfrey on their new mental health series set to air on May 21.

During his one-on-one with Oprah, Harry talked about the impact his wife Meghan Markle had in his life from the moment the two crossed paths.

Harry, who is the sixth in line on the royal family throne, said meeting Meghan made him realise something was broken in him that needed to be fixed.

According to Harry, having not dealt with his mother Princess Dian's death, made him grow with a lot of childhood trauma that was playing out in his relationships.

According to Harry, there was a lot of learning right at the beginning of his relationship with Markle and at one point, when the two argued, she mentioned that she needed to see someone.
