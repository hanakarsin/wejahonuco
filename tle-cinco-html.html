TLE Cinco - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Southern style hip hop artist who dropped a project called "Cinco 2.0" after previously putting out a 13-track EP. The mixtape was released by 300 Entertainment. He has released 3 full-length albums since 2019.

Before Fame

He began to establish himself as an artist to watch in 2018.

Trivia

His most listened to track on YouTube was called "Check Dis." He has accrued more than 150,000 followers on Instagram.

Family Life

His brother died on November 23, 2020. 

Associated With

Tee Grizzley was a guest on his song "All The Money."
