Eric Omondi crowned best African comedian for the 3rd time in a row

Publish date: 2024-06-19

- Eric Omondi is the best comedian in Africa again

- The father of one bagged the prize which was presented in the US during the African Entertainment Awards

- The content creator famed for his genius videos declared he is the president of comedy in the continent and plans to take it a notch higher

Once again, Eric Omondi has added a feather to his cap.

For the third time in a row, the content creator and entertainer has been crowned as the best comedian in Africa.

Imagine that! Eric announced the good news through a brief post on Instagram as he thanked his fans for their continued support.

The funnyman who has mastered the art of incorporating comedy in adverts said he was the undisputed champion and who are we to disagree?

Out of 53 countries and hundreds of comedians, the Kenyan treasure managed to defend his title and that alone deserved praise.

“Three years in a row, I am the president for life. We are about to take the continent to a whole new level,” he wrote in excitement.

The father of one was granted his award during the African Entertainment Award show which took place at the US.

As usual, team Eric rallied up in the comments and threw in congratulatory messages as they thanked him for always putting Kenya on the map.

No one was surprised by the win because by now, everyone knows who the sheriff of the town is.

In 2019, as per TUKO.co.ke reports, Eric returned home with two prizes.

He was named the best comedian in Africa yet again and even managed to be named the African king of comedy.

The proud and excited entertainer showed off his two trophies as he asked everyone else to bow down and enjoy his greatness.

Eric was celebrated for his efforts to crack ribs and spread laughter in a highly packed event held in the US.

"The president is now king. Every obstacle be it human or object, bow down,” Eric joked.
