FUNAAB acceptance fee and other payments for 2018-2019

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Prospective students of FUNAAB will be happy to know that FUNAAB acceptance fee and other payments have been released for the upcoming 2018-2019 session. Which FUNAAB payments should I keep in mind as a prospective student?

First of all, you need to remember that thinking about all the possible payments to the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta should start only after you know for certain that you are accepted into the University. In case you have already received the official admission notification, it’s time to know the payments.

How to pay FUNAAB fees

There are two ways to pay FUNAAB acceptance fee and other charges. The first one is good old bank transfer. You can find out from the FUNAAB registrar, which banks are accepted by the University and make a payment. For your convenience, iIt’s recommended that you use Remita Retrieval Reference at the official website. You need to select the option “Pay a Biller” and then provide the system with the details it requires from you.

  • Beneficiary: Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta
  • Service Name: Miscellaneous
  • (Ignore the GIFMIS code)
  • Amount: N30,750
  • Currency: Naira (NGN)
  • Payer: enter your full name
  • Phone: enter your working number
  • Email: enter your working gmail or yahoo email address
  • Select payment method: online banking, MasterCard, or bank transfer
  • Enter the CAPTCHA
  • Submit and receive the invoice
