I am not your wife - Uhuru tells off Joho

Publish date: 2024-08-02

- President Uhuru Kenyatta has told off Mombasa governor, Hassan Joho, for attempting to attend a function he was presiding

- Uhuru launched a ferry in Likoni on Monday, March 13, an event Joho had planned to attend

- He was blocked from accessing the town by GSU officers who escorted him back to his office

President Uhuru Kenyatta has once again told off Mombasa governor, Hassan Joho, after barring him from attending the launch of Mtongwe ferry services.

Speaking during the commissioning of the resumption of Mtongwe Ferry services, Uhuru castigated Joho for trying to force his way to the launch he was presiding.

"Mwambieni asituchezee...sisi tutamnyorosha (he should not joke with us...we will deal with him)," Uhuru said.
