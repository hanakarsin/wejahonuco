Who is the owner of Instagram now?

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Instagram is a mobile, desktop, and Internet-based photo-sharing application and service that allows users to share pictures and videos either publicly or privately to pre-approved followers. It was created by Kevin Systrom and his partner Mike Krieger as an upgrade of his first invention Burbn in October of 2010 and was later sold to Facebook Company in April 2012 for 1 billion dollars. Today Instagram is one of the biggest social media apps with billions of users. Well, here is a little trip on Instagram’s ownership journey.

Kevin Systrom

Has a net worth was estimated to be $1.3 billion. In April 2012, Systrom and his partner sold Instagram to Facebook at the cost of 1 billion.

Systrom took home 400 million out of the whole amount based on his 40% shares in the company.

When asked about his reasons for selling given that Instagram had such great potential, he said that Facebook had the best resources to grow the company and it was also committed to seeing it grow. He also said that even though he could have waited to sell it for a larger amount, he wasn’t quite too much into the monetary but more into the impact the app was and would make in people’s lives and the society at large.

Although Systrom sold the company to Facebook, he remained as the C.E.O and tasked with its growth and making of decisions in consultancy with the mother company. He launched an advertising platform on Instagram where companies pay to advertise their products in between photos.

In 2016, Forbes magazine ranked Systrom as a billionaire with an estimated net worth of $1.1 billion which came about as a result of Facebook stocks rising more than 500%.

Kevin Systrom, the CEO of Instagram tied the knot and married Nicole Schuetz in a private ceremony in October 2016. Nicole is the CEO of Sutro a clean-energy investment firm.

In February 2016, Systrom met with Pope Francis at the Vatican, where they discussed the power of images in uniting people "across borders, cultures and generations. He also presented him with 10 of the best Instagram images as a gift.

Mike Krieger

He co-founded Instagram with Kevin Systrom and even though Kevin is face of the company and its CEO Mike owned 10% shares in the company that saw him take home $100 million when the company was sold to Facebook.

Other shareholders

The remaining fifty percent is owned by other share holders who invested in the company’s starting capital

All this got a piece of the cake from the 1 billion dollars when the company was sold depending on their shares.
