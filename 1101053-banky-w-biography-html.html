Banky W biography: age, parents, wife, daughter, net worth, songs

Olubankole Wellington, commonly known by his stage name Banky W, is a super-talented Nigerian singer, rapper, actor, entrepreneur and politician. He is also the founder and owner of E.M.E Talent Management Company, formerly E.M.E record label.

Over the years, the singer has gained fame and global recognition for releasing hit songs. Some of his highly recognized songs include Heaven, Till My Dying, Whatchu Doing Tonight and Good Good Loving. Banky W is the husband of Nigerian actress Adesua Etomi.

Banky W biography

The rapper was born on 27th March 1981 in New York City. Banky W parents are George Bankole Wellington (father) and Momma W, commonly known as Mrs Wellington (mother). His father and mother are from Nigeria. His father hails from Badagry, while his mother hails from Calabar, Cross River State.

Even though the singer was born in the United States, his parents moved back to Nigeria when he was only five years old. He has four siblings, three brothers and one sister. There are no publicly known details about his siblings, such as their names.

The singer obtained his primary and secondary school education in Lagos before going back to the USA to further his education. He was awarded a scholarship to study at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in New York City.

Music career

The singer started building his career in the music industry while still undertaking his studies at the polytechnic. In 2002, the singer started a record label under E.M.E. He was also working at GlobalSpec. After completing his studies and staying in the United States for a while, the singer moved back to Nigeria.

He launched another record label in Lagos city. He began by signing several Nigeria artists such as Skales, Niyola, Wizkid and Shaydee. He had his breakthrough in the music industry in 2005 after releasing a studio album, Back in the Building. He also wrote the first theme song of Etisalat Nigeria, known as 0809ja for life.

The music label was converted to a talent management company. It focuses on marketing, advertising, public relations and brand events. The singer has released hit singles, studio albums, EPs, playlists and compilations albums.

Political ambitions

The actor has also ventured into politics. On 11th November, he announced his intentions for running for Lagos's Eti-Osa Federal Constituency seat in Nigeria's House of Representatives. He vied on the Modern Democratic Party platform that was formed in 2017.

Unfortunately, he lost the seat to Babajide Obanikoro of the All Progressive Congress. The elections were held on 23rd February 2019.
