Donald Trump Jr. (Business Executive) - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Executive vice president at his father's The Trump Organization who is famous as the first child of the 45th President of the United States Donald Trump.

Before Fame

He began working alongside his siblings Ivanka and Eric Trump at The Trump Organization.

Trivia

He is fluent in Czech. He has half siblings named Tiffany and Barron from his father's other marriages.

Family Life

He married model Vanessa Trump in November of 2005 and they divorced in 2018. He and Vanessa have children named Kai Madison, Chloe Sophia, Donald John III, Tristan Milos and Spencer Frederick. In 2018, he began dating Kimberly Guilfoyle. In 2021, the two were engaged. 

Associated With

His birth mother is Ivana Trump.
