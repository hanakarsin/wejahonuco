BBNaija All Stars: Leaving My Pregnant Wife Wasn't Easy

Publish date: 2024-06-02

Big Brother Naija All Stars housemate Chukwuemeka Okoye, better known as Frodd, has opened up on the reason why he left his pregnant wife to participate in the ongoing reality show.

Speaking on Friday, Frodd disclosed that the decision to leave his pregnant wife, Chioma, to come for the show wasn’t an easy one.

He added that he has the support of his wife to come for the show.

Frodd said, “I asked and said, ‘Won’t people say I’m irresponsible for leaving my pregnant wife to go and hustle?’”

Many were surprised to see Frodd in the Big Brother house days after announcing he was expecting a child with his wife.

The reality TV star got married to Chioma in February 2023.

Phyna Drags ‘Old Women’ At BBNaija All Stars Edition

Meanwhile, winner of Big Brother Naija Season 7, Josephine Otabor, popularly known as Phyna, on Thursday took a  swipe at some female housemates in the ongoing BBNaija All Stars edition.

Phyna, in a series of posts shared on her Snapchat, said she is ready to drag the ‘old women’ who are currently in the house, as they dragged her during her time on the show.

She also threw jabs at Venita Akpofure and asked her to leave Ilebaye alone.
