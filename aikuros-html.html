Hyoon - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-06

American digital illustrator and fan artist who would perhaps become most well known for her Aikuros Instagram account featuring selfies and her occasional artwork. She has earned more than 450,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She debuted a first Instagram post in August of 2014. It featured several drawings of the character Kageyama.

Trivia

She would use her hyoonk Twitch account to stream her drawings. She created a popular Tumblr blog as well. Her love for Pokemon and anime at an early age inspired her to start drawing. 

Family Life

She had a hamster named Kuma who passed away in September 2019.

Associated With

She and Israseyd both became known for their digital illustrations that they'd post online.
