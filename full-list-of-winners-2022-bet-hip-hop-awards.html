Full List Of Winners 2022 BET Hip Hop Awards

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Nigeria’s Afrobeats sensational singer/songwriter, Tems was among winners of the 2022 BET Hip Hop Award, taking home the Best Collaboration Category for contributing  to Future’s ‘Wait For U’ ft Drake.

Naija News reports that the BET Hip Hop Awards was held on 4th October 2022.

Tems earned three nominations for her contribution on Future’s ‘Wait For U’ namely- Song of the Year, Best Hip Hop Video, and Best Collaboration Award.

‘Wait For U’ edged out other nominees including ‘Family Ties’ by Baby Keem & Kendrick Lamar, ‘Way 2 Sexy’ by Drake Featuring Future & Young Thug, ‘Hot Shit’ by Cardi B, Kanye West & Lil Durk to win the Best Collaboration Award.

Fellow Nigerian superstar singer, Burna Boy who was nominated in the Video Director of the Year category for his self-directed music video, Last Last lost the award to Kendrick Lamar & Dave Free.
