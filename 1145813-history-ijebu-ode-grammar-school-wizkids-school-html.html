Ijebu Ode Grammar School, called the source of light, once provided education to many great individuals who have contributed and still contributing to the development of this great nation. From Law to politics, business, music, aviation, etc; Ijebu Ode Grammar school has raised many pioneers and industry leaders in Nigeria! Who are the top 10 most prominent and famous graduates of this school? Continue reading to find out more!

Ijebu Ode Grammar School

How can you describe Ijebu Ode Grammar School in a few simple words? People who studied there call it the Dawn of Life! It's one of the oldest and most reputable secondary schools in Nigeria. It Is also considered to be the oldest school in Ijebuland and the second oldest school in Ogun State.

The school was established on 20th of January 1913. The construction and establishing of the school were totally on the shoulders of the Anglican Mission in Nigeria! Nevertheless, the real history of the school started in 1892. In this year, the Ijebu people could openly declare their practice of Christianity. Therefore, as one of the next steps towards Enlightenment for them was the practice of western education.

The founder of the school was Rev. S.J. Gansallo. He received assistance and financial help from the Revd. J.A. Cole of Christ Church and Resident Pastors of St. Saviours Church. Cole. Rev. S.J. Gansallo connected his full-time pastor's job with his passion for teaching.

One of the first teachers of PA. D.M. Okugasa. He also contributed significant efforts to the development of the Ijebu Ode Grammar School Ogun. The foundation of the school was the fourteen students who became the first successful graduates.

The motto of the school is “Non-Nobis Domine”, translated as “Not unto us O Lord.” Therefore, the main idea of the motto is the transmitting the ideas of God's Enlightenment through education!

The school's anthem was introduced in 1953. It was composed by Rev. Canon S.R.S. Nicholas. The music for the anthem was provided by Fela Sowande. The main ideas that can be described in the school's anthem are the brotherhood among the students and God's light.

The main ideas of his business were stores which he opened in various points of Nigeria!

He is one of the most famous sports leaders in Nigeria. He is a Nigerian Taekwondo master and also one of the most prominent entrepreneurs in the Country. His main contribution is connected with the development of sport and youth movement in Nigeria.
