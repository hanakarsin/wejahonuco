October 11 Horoscope and Zodiac

Publish date: 2024-07-06

The Libra's ruling planet is Venus, but as you were born in the second Decan, or part, of the sign, Saturn and Uranus also lend some of their mysterious planetary power. Your desire for harmony, cooperation, and appreciation of beauty are all directly linked with Venus's influence. Likewise, your discipline, patience and caring for your fellow man can be linked to the powers of Saturn and Uranus. The uniqueness of your planetary influence makes you far more selfless than other Libra Decans. Although you have an undying commitment to help others, you often find it difficult to accept the same support from others. Try not to fear opening up about your concerns and worries, as you too can gain strength from others.
