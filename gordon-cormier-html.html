Gordon Cormier - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-01

Canadian actor famous for his roles in The Stand, Lost in Space and Two Sentence Horror Stories. In 2021, he was cast to play Aang in the live-action version of Avatar: The Last Airbender.

Before Fame

He made his acting debut in 2019 in Get Shorty.
