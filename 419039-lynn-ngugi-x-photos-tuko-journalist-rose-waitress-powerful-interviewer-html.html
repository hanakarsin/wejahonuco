Lynn Ngugi: 7 Photos of TUKO.co.ke Journalist Who Rose from Waitress to Powerful Interviewer

Publish date: 2024-08-08
  • Lynn Ngugi has been impacting the lives of many Kenyans through her role at TUKO.co.ke
  • She has won a number of humanitarian awards such as the I Change Nations Community Ambassador Award
  • Many people, including notable government personalities, have been lauding Lynn for her exemplary work

When Lynn Ngugi joined TUKO.co.ke in 2017, she was not sure it was a permanent home for her.

However, thanks to her good work, resilience, and consistency, the TUKO.co.ke senior producer and host is now one of the most popular media personalities in the world.

Well, just like many people, she came from far and went through a number of things to become a household name as far as media is concerned.

TUKO.co.ke highlights some of the high moments during her journey to stardom.

1. Lynn holds an Associate’s Degree in Mass Communication/ Broadcast Journalism from East Africa School of Media Studies.

2. The TUKO.co.ke producer has bagged various prestigious awards including recently when she was conferred with the I Change Nations Community Ambassador Award.

3. Lynn Ngugi always wanted to be a broadcast journalist, having graduated from media school but was unable to secure a job.

She, however, got an opening in Qatar where she worked as a waitress for a number of years and saved most of her salary, before returning to Kenya.

4. She has always wanted to have her own talk show and actually started one dubbed Kilimani Mums Talk Show months before joining TUKO.co.ke.

5. The celebrated journalist noted that she is inspired to do what she does by understanding that there is a possibility for a better tomorrow.

''What inspires me is the possibility of a better tomorrow and knowing that we have been able to impact so many lives together. So, there is still hope." Lynn said.
