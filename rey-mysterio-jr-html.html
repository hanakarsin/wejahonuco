Rey Mysterio - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Wrestler and 3-time world champion. He trained under Rey Misterio, Sr. and developed a high flying style and the finishing move, the 619 Kick, to honor San Diego.

Before Fame

He launched his career with ring names such as "La Lagartija Verde," and "The Green Lizard" as a Mexican luchador when he was fourteen.

Trivia

He won the Most Improved Wrestler Award in 1989 and in 1991, and became famous for his trademark Lucha Libre highflying style.

Family Life

He had a son named Dominik and a daughter named Aalyah with Angie Gutierrez, whom he married in 1996.

Associated With

He fought Randy Orton in a memorable match at WrestleMania XX.
