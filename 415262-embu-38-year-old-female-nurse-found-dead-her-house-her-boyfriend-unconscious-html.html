Embu: 38-Year-Old Female Nurse Found Dead in Her House, Her Boyfriend Unconscious

Police have launched a probe into an incident where a 38-year old woman was found dead in her house located in Kivaruka village, Embu county, under mysterious circumstances.

According to The Standard, the body of the woman identified as Wawira Nyaga, a nurse at Kithimu Health Centre, was discovered around noon, when she failed to receive her neighbours’ calls.

Preliminary investigations

According to preliminary investigations, the deceased's body did not have any visible injuries.

However, police said that she was strangled.

Her boyfriend, identified only as Munene, was found unconscious in a thicket not far from Wawira’s home.

Next to the 23-year-old man were bottles of beer and an empty processed juice can.

“He had vomited and was foaming from the mouth,” Charles Murimi, one of the eyewitnesses, said.

Eyewitnesses account

Marion Mukami, another witness, said they decided to reach Munene’s girlfriend, Wawira, on phone after they realised that he needed urgent medical attention.

“We realised that something was a miss after her phone went unanswered. One of the neighbours went to her house and discovered her door had been locked from the inside,” said Mukami.

Upon peeping through Wawira’s window, they saw her lying on the bed.

“Several attempts to wake her up were unsuccessful, prompting the neighbours to break down the door. Upon getting in, one of us, with a medical background, confirmed that Wawira had already died,” she added.

A reliable source privy to the matter said that the last time the couple was seen in public was on Wednesday, June 9, at around 3pm, when they visited a bar owned by the nurse.

The two are said to have dated for more than a year.

“The young man was jobless, and so he mostly depended on Wawira for upkeep,” a neighbour privy to the couple’s relationship said.

Previously married

Wawira was previously married, though she separated from her husband a few years ago. The former couple has a 10-year-old daughter.

Confirming the incident, Embu West Sub-County Deputy Police Commander Samuel Gitau said that Munene is undergoing treatment at a local hospital and will be interrogated upon discharge.

“All I can say is that at the moment, we are treating him as the key suspect in Wawira’s death,” said Gitau.
