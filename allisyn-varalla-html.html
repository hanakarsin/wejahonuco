Allisyn Varalla - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Main cast member on the Lifetime reality series Kim of Queens who works as a medical coder/biller while not filming the show.

Before Fame

She was a treatment coordinator for oral surgeons in the metro Atlanta area for 8 years.

Trivia

She is a coach at the Pageant Place.

Family Life

She married her husband Rick in the late 1990s and has a son named Brooks.

Associated With

Her older sister is Kim Gravel, the host of Kim of Queens.
