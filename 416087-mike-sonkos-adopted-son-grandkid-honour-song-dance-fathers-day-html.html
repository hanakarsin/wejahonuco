Mike Sonkos Adopted Son, Grandkid Honour Him Through Song and Dance for Fathers Day

Publish date: 2024-08-01
  • Mike Sonko's wife shared a video with a nice message to celebrate her hubby on Father's Day
  • From the video, his adopted son Satrin Osinya, granddaughter and many others, yet to be identified, could be seen dancing to celebrate their patriarch
  • The song they were dancing to hailed Sonko for his kindness and selfless acts

Former Nairobi governor Mike Sonko has received a Father's Day celebration messages from his son and grandkid as the world celebrates dads on June 20.

In a video shared by wife Primrose Mbuvi on Instagram, Mike Sonko got a wonderful dance performance to celebrate his day from his Nairobi home.

In the said clip, one could spot his granddaughter, Saumu Mbuvi's firstborn, and his adopted son Satrin Osinya as they danced together with others, yet to be identified, in a song praising the politician.

Sonko was praised for taking care of orphans, looking after widows and even starting the Sonko Rescue Team, which showed his good heart.

The song appeared to be a campaign track, and at the end, Primrose could be heard shouting "Happy Father's Day" as the rest joined in the cheers.

In her caption, she wrote:

"Happy Father’s Day to our sweet daddy @mike.sonko."

Mike Sonko celebrates Satrin's birthday

A few weeks back, Sonko celebrated his youngest adopted son Satrin's birthday with a touching post across his social media platforms.

On his ninth birthday, the flashy politician had kind words for the little boy and shared how proud he was of the young man.

This s what he shared on Facebook:

"When the Devil sent terrorists to kill your Mother in a church, he never thought he was a liar and defeated 2000 years ago. My Son, only a few parents are privileged to be blessed with a gifted son like you, every day we thank God for you. You are an extraordinary boy and a wonderful Son. May every dream and wish you have not only come true but also lead to an amazing future for you! Happy 9th birthday my son Satrine Osinya."
