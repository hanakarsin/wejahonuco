25 latest Ghana weaving shuku hairstyles in 2021 (photos and video)

Publish date: 2024-05-14

The current trend of cornrows might seem like a new one given its popularity among the young people and the hip hop community. However, cornrows are not a new phenomenon because this hairstyle has been around for ages. The popularity of this hairstyle can be attributed to its maintainability and versatility. Ghana weaving shuku style is one of the cornrow hairstyles that are currently trending. In this post we have 25 latest shuku hairstyles that you should not overlook.

The shuku hairstyle involves braiding the hair to form a hump on top of the head, and this makes them more attractive when compared to the normal cornrows. It is currently considered as one of the best protective hairstyles for those women that have short natural hair. You need to begin by creating small cornrow braids. You may add more synthetic hair as you go along in order to create bigger braids. You can also play around with different braid colours and adorn the hairstyle with beads and flowers to achieve the desired look.
