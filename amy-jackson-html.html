Amy Jackson (Model) - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 0001-01-01

British model and actress who gained notoriety due to her work in Indian movies. She won the Miss Teen World in 2008 and Miss Liverpool in 2009. In 2018, she joined the cast of The CW's Supergirl.

Before Fame

She signed a contract with Boss Model Management as a teen.

Trivia

She starred in the 2012 Hindi film Ekk Deewana Tha and the 2012 Tamil film Thaandavam.

Family Life

In September 2019, she and George Panayiotou, welcomed their son Andreas. The couple became engaged but later broke up. In 2022, she began dating actor Ed Westwick. She's previously dated both Prateik Babbar and Ryan Thomas.

Associated With

She made her film debut alongside Arya in the 2010 feature Madrasapattinam.
