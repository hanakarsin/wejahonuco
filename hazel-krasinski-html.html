Hazel Krasinski - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Daughter of former The Office actor John Krasinski and silver screen star Emily Blunt. Her parents have stated publicly that they strive to give their children normal upbringings, despite the attention that comes from famous parents. 

Before Fame

Her parents were married in 2010.

Trivia

John Krasinski stated that her birth helped him to direct his followup feature film The Hollars. Her father John gave her a shout-out while performing on Saturday Night Live in 2021.

Family Life

She has a younger sister named Violet.

Associated With

Her cousin, Matteo Oliver Tucci, is the son of her mother's sister Felicity Blunt and actor Stanley Tucci.
