Marko Terzo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-09

YouTube phenomenon with more than 5 million subscribers who is known for his custom art that he adds to clothing and sneakers. He often posted giveaways for his creations. He has often worked on Vans and Nikes but has also painted cars and iPhones.

Before Fame

He made his YouTube debut in December of 2017 with a video called "LOUIS VUITTON x VANS OLD SCHOOL - (Full Tutorial)."

Trivia

Several of his videos were watched more than 2 million times, however, his first to reach 3 million views was "Custom CRAYON Shoes!!" He partnered with Stadium Goods in August of 2019. He also made a video in January of 2021 where he painted custom boxing gloves for Logan Paul. 

Family Life

He was based out of California. His girlfriend bhadbaella first appeared on his YouTube channel in October 2020.

Associated With

He used the French Montana song "Unforgettable" for part of his debut YouTube video.
