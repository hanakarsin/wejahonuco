Mia Khalifa - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Lebanese-American social star who has gained followings on the platforms YouTube, Twitch and Instagram and is also known for hosting sports talk shows. She is a former adult film star and model who became an overnight sensation on adult film websites.

Before Fame

She graduated from the University of Texas at El Paso with a degree in history.

Trivia

She previously worked at a fast food restaurant. She served as a sports commentator on Complex News' web show Out of Bounds from 2017 to 2018.

Family Life

She was born in Beirut, Lebanon and moved to the United States in 2000. She married her high school sweetheart in 2011 and they later divorced in 2016. In early 2019, she announced her engagement to Swedish chef Robert Sandberg. She has been dating Jhay Cortez since 2021. 

Associated With

As an avid Florida State football fan, she attempted to recruit Braxton Miller to transfer schools from Ohio State to Florida State for the 2015 season.
