How to Find Spotify Wrapped to Listen to Your Fave 2023 Jams

Publish date: 2024-02-08

This is how to find your Spotify Wrapped to review everything you've listened to in the last year, and to see what everyone else has been playing too.

The Gist:

  • 2023's Spotify Wrapped dropped on Nov. 29.
  • You can find Wrapped by opening up your Spotify app and looking for it on the front page, or going to Spotify.com/Wrapped.
  • New features this year include "Sound Town" and "Me in 2023."

Swiping through your phone's camera roll is undeniably an efficient way to look back on what you did over the last year, but music never fails to make people feel incredibly nostalgic. Sometimes, music almost acts as a time capsule, transporting listeners back to what they were doing or feeling when they first tuned in. And that's precisely why we love when Spotify unleashes its end-of-year feature, Spotify Wrapped.

If you've never used Spotify Wrapped, it enables users to peruse their favorite playlists, artists, tunes, and more from the last year. So if you're looking to experience a serious blast from the past, here's what to know about Spotify Wrapped 2023.

How do you find Spotify Wrapped?

To check out Spotify Wrapped, just open up your Spotify app and look for it on the front page.

This year also marks the first time you can view your Wrapped from a web browser, if you head over to Spotify.com/Wrapped.

In general, your personalized Spotify Wrapped information will show the artists, tracks, and albums you listened to most, as well as how many minutes of music you listened to, top podcast genres, how many songs you "liked," and much more.

As for the new features this year? Along with some new metrics, you can check out "Sound Town," which assigns a city to you based on your listening tastes, or "Me in 2023," which connects you to one of 12 characters that you can share with others.

You'll also now see your Top 5 Genres "in a fun sandwich-inspired design." And you can even check our personalized messages from thousands of artists, including Dolly Parton and SZA.

Speaking of artists â€” they, along with songwriters and podcasters, can get their own "Artist and Creator Wrapped" experience to check out the ways their fans listened to them this year.

And for the first week of Wrapped, there's an AI DJ who can guide you through your personalized Wrapped. Plus, a feature called "Blend" lets you blend you and your pals' top songs into a shared playlist.

It's always fun to check Wrapped's new features every year. Some features that Spotify rolled out last year in 2022 include "Listening Personality" and "My Audio Day."

Spotify revealed the top artists for 2023 as well.

Spotify declared pop superstar Taylor Swift as the Artist of the Year, with 26+ billion streams in 2023. She dethroned Bad Bunny, who held the top spot since 2020 but is now in second place on the global list.

Meanwhile, "Flowers" by Miley Cyrus was the most streamed song on Spotify for the year, with 1.6 billion global streams. Joe Rogan's podcast also remains as the top podcast for the fourth year in a row.

Music is a great way to look back on the year, so get ready to experience ~all the feels~ through your headphones. We're ready to bid the year farewell with the best jam sesh imaginable with this year's Wrapped.
