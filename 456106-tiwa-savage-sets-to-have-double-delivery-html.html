Tiwa Savage Set To Have 'Double Delivery!

Publish date: 2024-02-05

The year 2015 has been a season of baby deliveries for lots of our entertainers.

On May 14,  Dr Sid’s wife, Simi Osomo, had the couple's first baby in London. The Nollywood actress, Uche Jombo, also had the couple's first baby, on Thursday, May 28, after having a miscarriage last year.

The sensational singer Omawumi, who had her baby shower in Nigeria on Sunday, April 12, before heading out to the United States, is expected to put to bed next, while the beautifully pregnant singer Tiwa Savage closely follows.

Based on the belief that a baby shower comes just a few weeks before delivery, Tiwa Savage, who had her baby shower on Sunday, May 17, will be the newest mother in the coming weeks.

However, it has been revealed that Tiwa has something up her sleeve as she plans a ‘double delivery.’  TheNetng reports that the singer, who is expecting a baby boy with Teebillz, has concluded plans to drop her second album on the day she puts to bed.

It was learnt that the singer plans to do this in order to remain relevant in the music industry while carrying out her duties as a mother.

Legit.ng wishes the beautiful singer a safe 'double delivery'!
