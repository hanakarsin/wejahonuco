Kinoo: Residents Worried as Storey Building Under Construction Cracks, Crumbles in Bits

Publish date: 2024-03-30
  • In a short video shared on social media, the building can be seen teetering as locals watch in disbelief
  • The structure has not collapsed, although residents fear that it could soon do so, blocking nearby pathways leading to their homes
  • No construction workers are trapped in the building located near Kinoo shopping centre in Kiambu county.

There was panic in Kinoo, Kiambu county on Friday, September 3, after an incomplete building partially sank into the ground, sending fear among locals.

In a short video shared on social media, the building can be seen wobbling as locals watch in disbelief.

The building is likely to collapse anytime soon

The structure has not collapsed, although residents fear it could soon do so, blocking nearby pathways leading to their homes.
