Dalexa - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Television actress who is widely recognized for her portrayal of Cecilia on the series Los Elegidos. She also played several characters on La Rosa de Guadalupe over the course of three years.

Before Fame

She made her television debut on Sueño de Amor in 2016. 

Trivia

She appeared on the three episodes of Como Dice el Dicho between 2016 and 2017.

Family Life

She wished her father a happy birthday in a May 2020 Instagram video.

Associated With

She starred alongside Macarena García Romero and Andrés Baida on Los Elegidos.
