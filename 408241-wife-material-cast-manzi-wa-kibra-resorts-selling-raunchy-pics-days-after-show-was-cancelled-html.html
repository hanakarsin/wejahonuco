Wife Material Cast Manzi Wa Kibra Resorts to Selling Raunchy Pics Days After Show Was Cancelled

Casts of comedian Eric Omondi's reality TV show, Wife Material were rendered jobless when the show was cancelled.

This meant trouble for some of the casts, including Manzi Wa Kibra who has resorted to selling her raunchy photo on a paying online platform.

Following the issues, she thought it wise to post her photos on a platform whereby people will have to be of age and pay KSh 5,000 to access her photos.

Manzi Wa Kibra had earlier on come out to claim her life had become tough after cancellation of Wife Material show.
