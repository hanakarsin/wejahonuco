Ex-BBNaija Star, Mercy Eke Buries Father In Imo

Publish date: 2024-08-07

The former reality star and winner of the season 4 edition of Big Brother Naija show, Mercy Eke, buried her late father on Wednesday.

Naija News recalls that Eke announced her father’s demise via her Instagram handle a month ago.

The reality star shared a photo of a lit candle in a dark background with a caption stating that she is so devastated to know that her father is no more.

She wrote: ‘I’m so devastated at the demise of my daddy. God, I don’t want to believe this is happening to me right now.’

Having mourned for four weeks, Mercy Eke with her family members on Wednesday, April 27, 2022, paid their last respect to the deceased.

Naija News understands that Eke’s father, Akwudike Eke died at the age of 70.

Eke took to her Instagram and Snapchat stories today to share clips of the burial ceremony.

She could be seen in the images accompanied by family members and friends as she laid to rest her father in her hometown in Imo State.
