CJ Salvador - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-18

Choreographer and dancer whose long list of credits includes several performances alongside Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez.

Before Fame

He attended North Central College in Naperville, Illinois as a marketing student. He began dancing at an early age, enlisting in tap dancing classes at 10 years-old.

Trivia

He has danced in episodes of The Voice and at award ceremonies such as the Billboard Awards and American Music Awards. On his popular YouTube channel, in addition to choreographed dances done in the studio, he also shares self-help and motivational tips, as well as travel vlogs from his personal life. 

Family Life

He is originally from Chicago and would relocate to Los Angeles, California. He's dated Delaney Glazer.

Associated With

He and fellow choreographer WilldaBeast Adams did choreography together to the Busta Rhymes song "Twerk It."
